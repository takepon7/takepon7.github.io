# gitai Playtest Feedback Form

Use these questions in Google Forms, Typeform, Tally, or any lightweight survey tool.

## Required Questions

1. Did you complete a first submission?
   - Yes
   - No

2. What device did you use?
   - Phone
   - Tablet
   - Desktop / laptop

3. What was your first reaction after the verdict?
   - I wanted to try again
   - I understood it but felt done
   - I was confused
   - Something broke

4. Did you open or share a result card?
   - Yes
   - No

5. Was the goal clear without outside explanation?
   - Clear
   - Mostly clear
   - Unclear

6. How did the drawing controls feel?
   - Smooth
   - Acceptable
   - Hard to use
   - Broken

7. Which feature did you notice or use?
   - Daily puzzle
   - Leaderboard
   - Ghost replay
   - Friend code
   - Share card
   - Appraiser comment
   - Pair proposal

8. What was the biggest problem?
   - Free text

9. What was the most shareable or funny moment?
   - Free text

10. May we use your screenshot or share card in launch materials if we ask you first?
    - Yes
    - No

## Operator Notes

Compare survey answers with in-app signals:

- `/v1/operator/playtest-feedback`
- `/v1/operator/content-reports`
- `reports/phase5/season_ops_<season-id>.md`

Treat survey confusion and in-app `bug` reactions as blockers until reproduced or intentionally accepted.
